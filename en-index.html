Welcome to my Website!

I am Philo, a freelance intercultural and ESD (Education for Sustainable Development) Educator. I live in Munich and I work all over Europe. My goal is to guide people on their way to a more sustainable life and to enable them to better understand the challenges of our global world.

I invite you to explore my website and to discover more about my work. From intercultural workshops to sustainable education projects, I offer a wide spectrum of services for children and young adults as well as for workers in different fields.

If you are interested in working together or if you have any questions, feel free to contact me. I am looking forward to work on interesting projects with you!
